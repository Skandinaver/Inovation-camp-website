What is Data Harvest?

Data Harvest is a proposed initiative to tackle the increasing dropout rates in schools across the world.
The program is designed to be a broad spectrum approach, being usable in both low and high development areas.
The project aims to give a good absence tracking system to give local governemnts information about where problems
occur, allowing them to target these areas with policies.

The project aims to deliver a combined approach, machine readable form templates will be distributed to teachers
in low infrastructure areas where internet and elecricity access is low. These forms can then be mailed in to a
central agency who registers the statistics to a national database. Not only will this allow for tracking of trends
but also easier scalability as infrastructure improves in areas.

The Data Harvest initiative hopes to secure UN funding to achieve it's goals. The project is designed to be low cost
and require minimal training.
